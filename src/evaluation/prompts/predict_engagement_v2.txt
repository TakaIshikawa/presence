For EACH tweet, provide scores on these criteria (1-10 scale):

1. HOOK_STRENGTH: Does the opening line create immediate tension, curiosity, or surprise?
   - 9-10: You physically cannot scroll past. "Wait, what?" reaction.
   - 7-8: Interesting enough to stop and read the whole thing.
   - 5-6: You might read it if you're not busy.
   - 1-4: Your eyes glaze over. Blends into the feed.
   - PENALIZE: Generic openings ("I've been thinking about...", "Here's why...")

2. SPECIFICITY: Is this grounded in a concrete situation or floating in abstraction?
   - 9-10: Names exact things — products, numbers, people, moments. You can picture it.
   - 5-6: Somewhat grounded but could apply to many situations.
   - 1-4: Pure abstraction. "AI is changing everything." Could be anyone's tweet.
   - NOTE: High specificity alone does NOT predict engagement. A very specific tweet about a niche topic will score low on other criteria.

3. EMOTIONAL_RESONANCE: Does this provoke a visceral reaction?
   - 9-10: You feel something — outrage, excitement, recognition, fear, amusement. You MUST respond.
   - 7-8: Strong "yes, exactly" or "wait, no" reaction. You'd like or reply.
   - 5-6: Mild head-nod. No urge to interact.
   - 1-4: Emotionally inert. Information without feeling.
   - THIS IS THE MOST IMPORTANT CRITERION. Weight it heavily in your final prediction.

4. NOVELTY: Would this tweet stand out in a feed full of similar accounts?
   - 9-10: Genuinely surprising framing. You'd screenshot this.
   - 7-8: Fresh angle on a known topic.
   - 5-6: Familiar territory, competent execution.
   - 1-4: Seen this exact take 50 times. "AI will change X" or "most people don't realize Y."

5. ACTIONABILITY: Would someone share, save, or quote-tweet this?
   - 9-10: You'd immediately send this to a friend or quote-tweet with your take.
   - 7-8: You'd bookmark it or like it publicly.
   - 5-6: You'd read and move on. No action taken.
   - 1-4: You'd scroll past.
   - KEY SHIFT: This is about shareability, not "can the reader apply this to their work."

6. PREDICTED_ENGAGEMENT: Your overall prediction (1-10).
   This is NOT an average of the above. Use your judgment about what drives engagement:
   - Emotional resonance matters most — tweets that make people FEEL something get shared
   - News and announcements with high stakes get outsized engagement
   - Contrarian or surprising takes get quote-tweeted (agreement AND disagreement drive engagement)
   - Well-crafted niche content often gets low engagement despite high quality — don't confuse quality with virality
